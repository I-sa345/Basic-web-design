The new tunel in Antioquia will be a complete revolution

By: Isabel Castañeda

The Túnel del Toyo, also called the Guillermo Gaviria Echeverry tunel is a massive proyect propossed and sponsored by the Goverment of Colombia and developed by the District of Medellin and the Department of Antioquia.


The idea of making this proyect has been referenced since 2005, capturing the attention of lots of investors around the country. Despite is a very revolucionary idea, the problem is the complex geografical location of the tunel, wich is planned to reach from Medellin to Uraba.


The transfer was signed and became final with the awarding of the supervision contract, wich was expected to be finalized by mid-December 2024. The work on the sector that will be transferred to us will begin in January 2025 and we estimate it will take 24 months
Said the Antioquia´s infrastructure secretary.
